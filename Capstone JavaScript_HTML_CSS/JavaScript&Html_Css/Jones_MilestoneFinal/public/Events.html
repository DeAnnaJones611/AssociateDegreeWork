From our Waterway Pub and Grill restaurant to our on-the-go Dog House snack bar, we provide a wide array of foods and beverage for players, tourists, and local customers to enjoy. Both the Waterway Pub and Grill and the Dog House snack bar are located on the green with panoramic views of the Intracoastal Waterway.
